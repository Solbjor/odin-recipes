Casado

Description

This is another classic dish of costarican cuisine that will leave you stuffed! It's called a "Casado" and it can be made in many different and unique ways!

There are steak casados, fish casados, chicken casados, and more! Each following a simple blueprint! This recipe page will focus on steak casado!

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Beans
  • Steak
  • Onion
  • Cilantro
  • Plantain
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Lime/Lemon
  • Salt
  • Pepper
    •

    Steps

    • Step 1:

      Cook the rice using your preferred method. I recommend using a stovetop pot method as the rice comes out much smoother and tastier.

    • Step 2:

      Cook the beans in a pot overnight, adding in various flavourings and vegetables of your choosing.

    • Step 3:

      Cook the steak on a pan on the stovetop, adding in spices to your liking. Chop up the onion into hollow circular slices for frying on the pan and toss them on alongisde the steak.

    • Step 4:

      Slice the plantain up vertically or horizontally and fry them in a pan till a crisp golden crust forms on the edges whith the interior remaining soft and bright gold.

    • Step 5:

      Chop up the lettuce and tomatoes and mix in onion or other veggies to your liking to create a salad.

    • Step 6:

      Organize the rice and beans onto the plate alongside the salad, place the steak, fried plantains and fried onions with the rest and sprinkle lime/lemon on it!
