Gallo Pinto

Description

This recipe is a staple of costarican cuisine and is found throughout the entire country! Breakfast, lunch, or dinner, this dish is always a tasty meal to include in your day. It is also really easy to make!

Out of all the dishes you could try when visiting Costa Rica, this is one I highly suggest you do not skip over, as missing the authentic taste and cooking process is a tragedy.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Beans
  • Bell Pepper
  • Onion
  • Cilantro
  • Salsa Lizano
  • Salt
  • Pepper
    •

    Steps

    • Step 1:

      Cook the rice using your preferred method. I recommend using a stovetop pot method as the rice comes out much smoother and tastier.

    • Step 2:

      Cook the beans in a pot overnight, adding in various flavourings and vegetables of your choosing.

    • Step 3:

      Chop up the bell pepper, cilantro, and onion into small square pieces. Set them aside for when the mixing begins.

    • Step 4:

      Add the rice and beans into a pot set to low heat. Toss in the chopped up bell pepper, onion, and cilantro. Add in Salsa Lizano in variable amount depending on your preference and mix it all.

    • Step 5:

      Turn off the stove and serve with eggs and tortillas!
